About

Portal can take you anywhere within a 1000 km radius, or 1500 clicks. We have the longest range, the fastest travel time, and the best service. We offer full refunds for glitchy trips, and compensate for damages.

Regretfully, we are not capable of inteplanetary teleportation as of yet, so we recommend portaling up to a Spaceport Station and taking a shuttle to your destination for a quick trip, or a starship for a more scenic view.

Splinching

Splinching, or having objects or body parts left behind or lost in transit, is extremely rare. Nevertheless, we compensate for the lost item or teleport it in an additional trip for no charge. We also offer complementary prosthetics, courtesy of Bionics Inc..

Picasso Effect

The Picasso Effect occurs when an object does not maintain its proper position throughout the teleportation process, and instead migrates away from its original position or disconnects from its host entirely. To have this fixed free of charge, simply visit the nearest Portal customer service station and wait for relocation.

Error 404: Destination Not Found

We are not responsible for incorrect entries, but if a proven glitch occurs, we can teleport you to your true destination free of charge.
If the Portal station you were traveling to experiences an error while you were in transit, you will be relocated to the nearest station, and alternate transportation will be provided.
