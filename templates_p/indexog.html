About

I am an experienced and curious inspector with over three years experience as Inspector General in a Catsville Condo. I am responsible for searching all boxes, bags, and anything else that comes into the building. During my tenure in this role we have not had a single receipt enter the building without me finding it and batting it around: I am known for completing my searches in a timely and thorough manner.

I also have deep skills at swatting at objects, with a specialty in hair ties. I can run from the living room to the office to catch a hair tie in 1.7 seconds. These skills are supported by my background in climbing, which enables me to get even those hair ties that go on top of the cabinets.
